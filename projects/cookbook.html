Bavarian Cookbook

Des werd da schmecka - Traditionelle Rezepte aus Bayern

Des werd da schmecka

I grew up in Bavaria, where I learned how vital cooking fresh and varied food is. My intention with this project was to design a traditional, Bavarian cookbook, filled with my Mother’s and Grandmother’s old and collected recipes to keep them safe. I meticulously assorted them and created this summary of dishes from my culture. Through photography, I wanted to give the beholder a genuine and honest feeling for Bavarian kitchen and tradition.
