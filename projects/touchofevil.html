Film Noir - Touch of Evil

Touch of Evil

I created a new title sequence for the movie Touch of Evil under the premise that the film will be updated for a modern audience. The movie genre is called Film Noir, a style of filmmaking characterized by fear, mistrust, bleakness, and despair. Therefore the overall atmosphere and composition of the updated title sequence aspires to be very dark and creates tension and discomfort in the viewer.
